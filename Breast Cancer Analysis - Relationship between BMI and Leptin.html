Breast Cancer - Relationship between BMI and Leptin

This is an interactive web app that covers a few of the well-known mathematical models for neurons. You can select the type of model and adjust its threshold, voltage reset, and the amount of input and be able to see the changes of the neuron through the plot.

This model is a regression model that analysis a breast cancer dataset. The dataset contains data based on the age, BMI, Leptin, Glucose, and etc. levels.

One of the difficulties I faced during my project is choosing what data is able to fit the normal data distributio and would also makes sense to compare. I started to compare the different levels to the age but they were all over the place. I got creative and wanted to see if there was any relationship between the levels themselves. If the inccrese or decrease of one affect the other leves. By pure luck my second choice was to compare the BMI and the Leptin level and they fit my requirements for the data distribution. I then researched on what Leptin is and what is its relationship with BMI and found some really good research articles.
